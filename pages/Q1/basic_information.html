Streaming

Definition of Streaming

Streaming is a type of media that is delivered and consumed in a continuous manner from a source. It’s a process of transmitting or receiving data, especially video and audio material over a computer network as a steady, continuous flow, allowing playback to start while the rest of the data is still being received.


How Does Streaming Work?

It works by breaking down the data packets that constitute the video or audio data and interpreting each play as a video or audio in the player on the user’s device. Streaming allows users to view such content continuously and enjoy a seamless viewing. Instead of the entire media file being downloaded first, the content is transmitted in data packets a few seconds at a time and stored on the user’s device to be played there remotely.

Types of Streaming Content

There are two types of streaming: live streaming and recorded streaming, which differ in how they are created and presented. Regular streaming is already recorded before it is streamed across the internet, so you can pause and play it back at any time, whereas live streaming is done in real time, which means there is no pre-recording and it is played in real time, so when you pause, you will not be able to resume where you left off. This streaming content types includes video games, movies, , music videos, educational videos, podcasts, webcasts, interviews, and so on. All of these content types serves different purposes and covers different topics.

Platforms Used for Streaming

Streaming Platforms are online service providers that can host streams and deliver streaming content over the internet. Some of the most commonly used streaming platforms are:

Twitch Twitch emphasizes live streaming and interaction with others.
Facebook Facebook emphasizes streaming to friends and family.
YouTube YouTube emphasizes monetization, and it is one of the most used streaming platforms for video.
Spotify Spotify is focused on streaming music and radios, and it is one of the most popular streaming platform for music.
Soundcloud Soundcloud is also focused on streaming music while supporting monetization for its musical artists.
Netflix Netflix is focused on streaming movies and series. However, a subscription plan is required to use the platform.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

  • A good stream is able to fully capture the audience’s attention.
  • A good stream effectively serves their purpose in either entertaining, educating, informing or persuading the audience.
  • A good stream is consistent in its content and does not deviate from the main subject of the stream.
  • A good stream is respectful and inclusive to their audience, it should not seek to disrespect or alienate a person or group.
  • A good stream should be able to communicate well with their audience, it should be interactive and engaging as viewers often prefer to connect with the streamer.
