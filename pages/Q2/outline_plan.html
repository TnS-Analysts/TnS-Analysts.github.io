Promotional Plan Outline

  • Main Objective
    • To be able to accumulate at least 50 viewers at the day of the stream
  • Target Audience
    • High School students aged 13 to 18 who are interested in music and its applications to productivity
  • Strategies
    • Distribution
      • The poster and teaser video will be distributed via Gmail and social media platforms.
      • The promotional materials will be distributed through our Facebook and Instagram accounts and in our APEC email accounts across all APEC students in hopes of attracting as many students as possible to participate in and support our stream.
    • Poster
      • The poster must include crucial information about the stream, including the main topic, (tentative) date and time, and the social media platforms in which the stream will take place.
      • The poster must contain a key message that would persuade potential viewers about the benefits they would get from the upcoming stream.
      • The poster’s art style must be inspired by Classical or Vaporwave music, and its colors must be in line with the group’s color scheme.
    • Teaser video
      • The teaser video must clearly showcase what the stream will be all about and why it is worth the viewers’ time.
      • The teaser video must include unique elements that will cause the stream to stand out from other streams among the potential audience, which may include appealing to the viewers’ interests or incorporating humorous elements into it.
      • The teaser video must also include the crucial information mentioned in the poster.
